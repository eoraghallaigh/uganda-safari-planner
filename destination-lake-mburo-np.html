Lake Mburo NP

⏱ 1 Night Stop 🌤 Sunny and dry, ~27°C (81°F).

Destination Overview

Lake Mburo National Park is a compact gem characterized by acacia woodlands, grassy valleys, and a series of lakes. It is the closest savanna park to Kampala/Entebbe and is unique for being the only park in western Uganda containing impalas and massive elands. Lacking elephants and lions, Lake Mburo is ideal for walking safaris, horseback safaris, and mountain biking, allowing visitors to get remarkably close to zebras, giraffes, and buffaloes.

Possible Activities

✔
Walking safari to get up close with zebras, giraffes, and impalas.
✔
Sunset boat cruise on Lake Mburo to spot hippos and waterbirds.
✔
Morning game drive through acacia woodlands.
✔
Horseback riding safari across the savanna plains.
✔
Mountain biking safari along designated park wilderness trails.
✔
Guided night game drive to search for leopards and spotted hyenas.
✔
Sport fishing on Lake Mburo for tilapia.
