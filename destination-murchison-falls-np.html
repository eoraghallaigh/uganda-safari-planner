Murchison Falls NP

⏱ 3 Nights Stop 🌤 Hot and dry, ~30°C (86°F).

Destination Overview

Murchison Falls National Park is Uganda's largest protected area, bisected by the Victoria Nile. The park's crowning jewel is the dramatic Murchison Falls, where the entire Nile squeezes through a narrow 7-meter gorge before plunging 43 meters into the 'Devil's Cauldron.' This park offers classic savanna safaris, home to elephants, giraffes, lions, and leopards, alongside river cruises teeming with hippos, massive Nile crocodiles, and rare shoebill storks.

Possible Activities

✔
Sunrise game drives in the northern sector savanna plains.
✔
Boat safari up the Victoria Nile to the base of the falls.
✔
Scenic hike to the 'Top of the Falls' to experience the thunderous waters.
✔
Hot air balloon safari over the savanna at sunrise.
✔
Delta boat cruise downriver towards Lake Albert for shoebill stork sightings.
✔
Guided night game drive to spot leopards, hyenas, and nightjars.
✔
Chimpanzee tracking in the adjacent Budongo Forest.
